# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

In the interest of fostering an open and respectful research software community,
contributors and participants in this project are expected to engage in a
professional, constructive, and inclusive manner.

## Expected behavior

Examples of expected behavior include:

- being respectful in discussion and review,
- offering constructive feedback,
- being open to correction,
- and focusing discussion on the software, documentation, and research workflow.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory
language, bad-faith disruption, or any conduct that makes participation hostile
or unsafe.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests,
and discussions related to the repository.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project
maintainer directly.
